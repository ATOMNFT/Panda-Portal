How to use:

  • 🧾 NFT Scanner: Paste Ethereum wallet addresses (comma or newline separated) and click Scan Wallets to see which NFTs they hold.
  • 📊 Token Tracker: Enter any wallet address to check its Panda Token balance on the Abstract blockchain.
  • 🌓 Use the theme toggle to switch between Light and Dark modes anytime.

🔍 What does it check?
This app checks the three newest official NFT contracts from @PandaMania_NFT to verify if the wallet(s) you provide are holding official PandaMania NFTs.

🔒 Your safety matters:
This app does not connect to your wallet, ask for permissions, or interact with private keys. All lookups are done using public blockchain data, and your assets remain completely safe. You can use it anonymously and securely.
